Board of Directors

Our Directors are passionate advocates of the PWAN's vision and work on behalf of the global network to achieve our strategic goals

Chairman

Dr Augustine Ozioma Onwumere

Dr. Augustine Ozioma Onwumere is a Co-Founder of the PWAN GROUP.

He sits as the Chairman of the PWAN Group which is the largest and widest Real Estate Network in Nigeria with estates and offices spread across several States of the federation…Lagos, Ogun, Abuja, Owerri in Imo State, Port Harcourt in Rivers State, Asaba and Warri in Delta State, Anambra State, Enugu, Uyo in Akwa Ibom State, Ibadan in Oyo state and many more.

He is also the CEO of Five (5) service and product Companies in Lagos, Abuja, Asaba and Port Harcourt. Marc Austine as he is generally called was born to a wealthy family in Lagos, Nigeria and due to his relentless nature amidst his siblings decided to tread the path of sales at an early age of eight. He was encouraged by his Father’s quest and legacy for a successful lifestyle through hard work and faith in God.

He is a well-loved Lagos-based Real Estate and Human Empowerment Strategist with special focus in Sales and Development. He is an enthusiastic and dedicated leader with both significant and practical experience in a working environment. He is quick in grasping new ideas and concepts and very innovative to proffer realistic solutions to problems. He has demonstrated the highest levels of motivation and has a strong ability to perform effectively even within a highly pressurized working environment.

Augustine is a dynamic leader, consistently sited for unbridled passion for work. His energy and enthusiasm as a leader who has broken all barriers to get to the peak cannot be quantified. He is a force to be reckoned with. His pro-activeness and drive for success have established PWAN GROUP on a foundation of reliable, resourceful and excellent organization for her clientele. He has received honorary awards from various institutions and led the Group to receiving various awards such as African Quality Achievement Award, Global Quality Excellence Award and so on. He is never tired of investing in New Dreams, creating jobs for over 150 Young Nigerians excluding the over 30 thousand Independent Real Estate Professionals generally called Consultants working with PWAN; his vision is stemmed around personnel development and target achievements.

Augustine Onwumere, a graduate from University of Lagos Akoka, has attended several courses in Lagos Business School and other learning institutions and continues to continuously develop himself to be able to empower others. He is married with three children to Jayne Obioma Onwumere; the woman he fondly refers to as the brain behind the business that has placed him where he is.

GROUP MANAGING DIRECTOR

Dr Jayne Obioma Onwumere

Jayne Obioma Onwumere is the Group Managing Director of The PWAN GROUP. PWAN is an acronym for Property World Africa Network.

She leads a team of 17 Managing Directors with 110 direct Staff spread across 17 Affiliate Companies that make up the PWAN Group and over 30 thousand Independent Real Estate Professionals generally called Consultants. At a time when Nigeria’s employment market was dwindling and some families losing their homes due to lack of funds to pay rent, Jayne and her darling husband Augustine Onwumere received a God given vision to make home ownership dream a reality for as many people as possible.

This dream birthed a Real Estate Network Marketing Company in March 2012 with the Pioneer Company known as PWAN Homes. PWAN HOMES is acronym for Property World Africa Network Home Ownership Made Easy Scheme. PWAN pioneered the first Real Estate Network Marketing Business in Nigeria, Africa and in the World (to the best of our knowledge) providing opportunities for hundreds of thousands of Nigerians to earn consistent Income as Real Estate Entrepreneurs.

In the history of Nigeria, we have not experienced more people in Real Estate than we have now and it is all due to this novel idea birthed by this Couple even at a time when they were homeless. With focus, determination and hardwork, The PWAN Team today (comprising of Staffs, Partners and Consultants) are engaging with both public and private sectors, Individuals and Nigerians in the diaspora to bring massive change in the life of people. This has strengthened synergies and yielded positive outcomes for the sector. In 2017, Mama Africa as she is fondly called single handedly empowered 50 women to start up small business venture or to improve existing ones with Fifty Thousand Naira each through her Empowerment for all Women Program.

This she intends to do every year for as long as the Lord enables her to do so. She has organized several outreaches to Orphanages with a lot of provisions and monetary gifts. Together with her husband, she pays school fees for so many children, pays house rent for many and keeps a Completely Open-Door policy that has made many refer to her as Mummy, Mama Africa etc. The work done by PWAN in just a little over Six (6) years has inspired so many people to venture not just into real estate business by using the PWAN Model but into other businesses.

People now understand you do not necessarily need money to venture, you need faith, you need focus and you need tenacity. And mostly, you need to have love for people and a desire to empower people to become the best they can be. Jayne Obioma Onwumere, a Lifelong Learner, is an Alumni of Lagos Business School. She is married to her Best Friend, a man she believes God used to birth relevance in her life and they are blessed with three lovely children.

MANAGING DIRECTOR/CEO

Odusanya Mukaila Odutola

Odusanya .M. Odutola is the Managing Director of PWAN Lekki.  He coaches and trains people on becoming independent marketers through real estate.

With more than Six years of marketing and senior management experience in the real estate sector, Odutola brings insight and expertise to his position as Managing Director of PWAN Lekki. 

He was specially recognised and given an award of excellence in Real Estate and Economic development by JCI Lagos Coral.

A humble goal getter who hails from Odogbolu local government area of Ogun State.  He had his primary education at Idi-Odo primary school, Gbagada and Anthony Village High School both in Lagos state.  He proceeded to Rivers State University of Science and Technology where he received a degree in accounting.

 He has worked in reputable establishments such as Arksego Security Company, Halogen Security Company, Sichuan Lida Petroleum and Machinery Engineering Company, Baslugde Oil and Gas Limited in Port Harcourt. 

Also worked with Villa Angelia ltd and Uber before joining The Life-changing PWAN HOMES in 2014. 

He joined as a partner, got training and certifications in real estate from PWAN GROUP in countries like Accra Ghana, United Arab Emirates (UAE) and the United States of America.

EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR/COO

Nnadi Daniel

NNADI Daniel Osonduagwuike (Lagos Salesman) is a seasoned real estate Investment Advisory Professional, a thought leader, and an active citizen.

Daniel graduated with a BA. (Hons.) In Fine and Applied Art with options in (Graphics and Advertising) from Imo State University. A PMP certified project manager, a Certified Negotiation Specialist, a trained Ec-council CRM, and alumni of JCI leadership Academy. He currently serves as the 2021 President of Junior Chamber International, Lagos Coral arm. He holds a diploma in Ministerial leadership from Proof Producers Bible School and currently pursuing an Oak seed Executive Leadership Course at Institute for National Transformation.

In 2010 he started his careers in HR department of United Bank for Africa HQ, Marina and led a team of colleagues in UBA Nigeria staff guarantor’s validation exercise. In 2012, he saw a need to express his intent in real estate sector and took up the opportunity by joining the sales force of PWAN. Through the platform created by PWAN, Daniel gained momentum and has today evolved with rooted knowledge in real estate by servicing pool of clientele both within and in the diaspora.

He is a transformational leader whose quest for National consciousness and personal development are paramount. He remained career focused and believes in the philosophy of the “the road less traveled”. He loves reading and obsessed with good music.

His Pulse: Advocating the oasis of the first world mindset in the third world Nations.
